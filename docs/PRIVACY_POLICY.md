# Privacy Policy - Maza Panchang

Effective date: 26 April 2026

Maza Panchang (माझं पंचांग) values user privacy.

## 1. Data we collect

We do not require account creation.

The app may access:

- Approximate/current location (only if user grants permission) to auto-select nearest Maharashtra district.
- Local app settings (district selection, font mode, notification choices), stored on device.

## 2. Data usage

- Location is used only to detect nearest district for Panchang calculation.
- Settings are used only to personalize app behavior.

## 3. Data sharing

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising.

## 4. Notifications

If user enables notifications, the app schedules local notifications for daily Panchang and special festival/upwas reminders.

## 5. Storage

Most app data is processed on-device.

## 6. Security

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no method is 100% secure.

## 7. Children

The app is intended for general audience. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data from children.

## 8. Changes to policy

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected with a new effective date.

For privacy questions, contact: [support@mazapanchang.app](mailto:support@mazapanchang.app) (replace with your real address)
